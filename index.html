Volunteer
at horse shows

Come volunteer on Sundays at horseback riding events! The shows take place at the Bordeaux Equestrian Center, near Bordeaux, every Sunday from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm. There are many ways to volunteer, you can help at the restaurant, groom horses, or put up the obstacles. To participate, complete the form below during the week before the event.

Join us!

If you are interested in coming to help us at this event, please fill out the following form. You can complete this form until two days before the event, and you will receive further information and a planning the day before the event. Volunteering is a great way to participate and have fun at the same time!
